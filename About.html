Poem reveals me:


In the rhythm of life, Govind stands tall,
With each word he speaks, he captures all.
A blend of wisdom, and stories untold,
In his presence, hearts unfold.

With a glance so deep, like an ocean vast,
Every moment with him, an adventure cast.
In the language of kindness, and the charm of grace,
Govind, a gem, in every place.

With a smile that lights up the darkest night,
In his aura, everything feels right.
With each step he takes, a melody divine,
Govind, the darling of time.
